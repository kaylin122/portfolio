SMOKE

A children’s book following a girl who wonders into the woods after seeing a thin cloud of smoke.

SMOKE is based on the feeling of becoming entranced in a fire. On a cold fall night the crackling of wood, smell of smoke, and bright, blazing heat of a fire grazing your skin while surrounded by darkness is an other worldly feeling. It’s almost as if nothing else exists except you and the fire.
