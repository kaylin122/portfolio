2022 Bowe House Press Calendar

A 12 month calendar hand produced by members of the Bowe House press.

This 12 month calendar was created as part of the annual tradition where each member designs and carves their own unique design into a linoleum block. Most choose a reductive method of carving each layer of color from the same linoleum block, printing, then repeating for each color. This process is unique in that once you carve over a layer you cannot go back to make more prints. A total of 36 limited editions were created.

12"x6" Letterpress & Linocut Printed
