PACK

PACK is a memory game where you are matching items to pack in your backpack.

Each illustration depicts an actual item that I keep in my backpack day-to-day. To play, shuffle cards, lay each card face down in a grid, flip over two cards at a time aiming to match the two cards, and flipping back over two cards that do not match. Then, repeat until all cards have been paired up.

74 cards (including instruction cards) created in Procreate, Illustrator, and Canva
